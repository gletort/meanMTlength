Mean MT length

Calculation of mean MT length for the MT dynamics parameters, according to formula from Verde et al., 1995):
Growth rate: \(\mu m.s^{-1}\)
Shrinkage rate: \(\mu m.s^{-1}\)
Catastrophe rate: \(s^{-1}\)
Rescue rate: \(s^{-1}\)

Mean MT length : \(\mu m\)

MT rigidity

Conversion of MT persistence length to MT rigidity (R=kT * Lp)
Temperature in kT: \(pN.\mu m\)
Persistence length: \( \mu m\)

Rigidity: \( pN.\mu m^2\)
